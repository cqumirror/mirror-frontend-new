The upstream source for the Ubuntu Kylin distribution, `cdimage.ubuntukylin.com`, has been removed, and the mirror site has been unable to successfully synchronize this repository for several months. After discussion, we have decided to remove this source. We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience.
